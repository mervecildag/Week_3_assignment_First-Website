A country that brings a feast to the eyes with every shade of green and its protected nature.

8.6 million people live in Switzerland on almost 41,300 km². The state is thus one of the most densely populated states in Europe, with the population concentrated in the central plateau, the basin zone between the Jura and the Alps, and in southern Ticino. The eight largest cities and economic centers are Zurich, Geneva, Basel, Bern, Lausanne, Winterthur, Lucerne and St. Gallen.
